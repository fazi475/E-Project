Periodontics: Management of Advanced Periodontitis

1.Case Details :-

Patient: Male, 45 years

Chief Complaint: "My gums bleed when I brush my teeth."

Findings

Bleeding on probing, pocket depths of 6–8 mm in molar regions.

Generalized bone loss visible on radiographs.

Diagnosis

Generalized Stage III Periodontitis

Treatment Plan:-

1.Initial Phase:
Scaling and root planing.
Oral hygiene instructions

2.Surgical Phase:
Flap surgery in severe areas..
Use of guided tissue regeneration (GTR).

3.Maintenance Phase::
Periodontal maintenance therapy every 3–4 months.

Outcome:-

Significant reduction in pocket depths and improvement in gum health.

Orthodontics: Crowding Correction

1.Case Details :-

Patient: Male, 16 years

Chief Complaint: "My teeth are crowded, and I don’t like my smile."

Findings

Severe crowding in the maxillary arch.

Class I molar relationship.

Diagnosis

Dental crowding in the maxilla.

Treatment Plan:-

1.Extraction:
Scaling and root planing.
Oral hygiene instructions

2.Fixed Appliances:: :
Flap surgery in severe areas..
Use of guided tissue regeneration (GTR).

3.Retention Phase:
Periodontal maintenance therapy every 3–4 months.

Outcome:-

Significant reduction in pocket depths and improvement in gum health.
